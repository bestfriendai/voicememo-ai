# VoiceMemo AI - Market Research

## Market Opportunity

### Voice Recording App Market
- **Global Market Size (2024)**: $4.2 billion
- **Projected CAGR**: 12.3% through 2030
- **Key Drivers**: Remote work, content creation, note-taking automation

### Target Demographics
1. **Professionals** (35%): Meeting notes, ideas capture
2. **Students** (25%): Lecture recording, study notes
3. **Content Creators** (20%): Script ideation, voice memos
4. **Personal Users** (20%): Daily reminders, personal notes

### Problem Statement
- Existing voice memo apps lack intelligent transcription
- Manual transcription is time-consuming ($0.25/min average)
- Users want searchable, organized voice notes
- AI-powered transcription is becoming expected

### Opportunity
VoiceMemo AI combines simple recording with powerful AI:
- Free tier: Basic recording
- Premium: Unlimited transcription, summarization, smart tags

1. **Simple, focused UI** - One-tap recording
2. **AI-powered organization** - Auto-summaries and tags
